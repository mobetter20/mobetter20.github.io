From the Clerk's Office — Spring 2026

The Avian Municipal District serves the residents of Sycamore Lane, Birch Court, Municipal Oak, and surrounding branches. The Clerk's Office maintains public records, processes filings, and provides access to district services. It does not provide opinions on who was right. It has opinions. It does not provide them.

This spring has been active. The Court has processed four cases this term, including two nest abandonments, a noise matter under compliance review, and a domestic dispute about humming. Solo nesting consultations with approved contractors are up.

The fax line remains not operational. Please do not ask again until autumn.

— T. Nuthatch, Clerk of Court

Frequently Requested Information
How do I file a nest abandonment claim?
Petitions may be submitted through the eCourt Public Portal or in person during business hours. Documentation should include a description of the nest, the date of departure, and evidence of where the respondent went. "He knows what he did" is not evidence.
How do I file a noise complaint?
File with the Clerk's Office. Include dates, times, description of the noise, and its apparent purpose. Residents describing the noise as "you'd have to hear it" are advised that the Clerk has heard it. The Clerk lives on Municipal Oak.
Is SecondNest affiliated with the district?
No. The Clerk's Office lists it because not listing it resulted in more questions than listing it.
Can I attend a court hearing?
Hearings are open to the public. Seating is limited. Spectators who have already formed opinions are asked to keep them at foraging volume.
