The district's law

The Ordinance Code

Cited often and read rarely. Reproduced below in full.

Ord. 9.3(a)
Noise disturbance
No resident shall vocalize between 9:00 PM and 6:30 AM. The Court has found that 6:29 AM is before 6:30 AM.
Ord. 14.7(b)
Public records access
The records of the District are open to the public. Copies are made at the window, one per party.
Ord. 22.1
Perch occupancy
A perch accommodates the birds upon it. Seating is not assigned.
Ord. 31.4
Abandoned property
Property left unclaimed for ninety days may be adjudicated abandoned and released for general use. The drawer is not indefinite.

Frequently requested

Information

How do I file for nest abandonment?
Through the eCourt Public Portal or at the window. Include the nest, the date of departure, and evidence of where the respondent went. "He knows what he did" is not evidence.
May I observe a proceeding?
Three matters are open to gallery observation through the eCourt Portal. Observation is not attendance and does not count toward community service hours.
Where did the respondent go?
The Clerk's Office does not know. In one matter the Court made no finding as to where the respondent went — only that he has not come back.
