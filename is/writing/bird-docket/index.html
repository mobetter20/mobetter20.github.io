What's online in the district.

Public sites and services from the Avian Municipal District. Some are official. Most are not. All are maintained by someone, somewhere, with varying degrees of enthusiasm.

Avian Municipal District Official portal

Clerk-maintained district portal for services, notices, public records, and the official version of events.

Avian Municipal Nest Court Court portal

Case lookup, filings, and calendars. The municipal belief that a portal improves morale.

The Perch District service

Community chat maintained by the Clerk's Office. For municipal coordination purposes.

Karen Hawk, Attorney at Law Licensed practitioner

Solo practice for nest abandonment, no-perch orders, and the hour after a bird says they need space.

SecondNest New

Text-based personals for district residents. Free. No photos. For birds who know better now.

The Municipal Coo Newspaper

Local notices, public embarrassment, classifieds, and the weather as reported by rumor. Published weekly.

Proceedings Registry Court records

What was said, what was struck, and what somehow entered the record anyway.

Bird of Letters External

Statements made outside official channels, which is to say most statements.
