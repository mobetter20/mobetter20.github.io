Feeling Burnout? Relax And Reprogram Your Mind With LinkedIn Hypnotherapy

Use the following verbal repetition in our job postings to achieve a corporate state of mind

Originally published on Medium on September 16, 2022. View original

  • You are self-driven, data-driven, and goal-driven.
  • You are a leader and a team player.
  • You speak the language of trends and ideas.
  • You have a hacker’s mindset.
  • You are extremely comfortable in ambiguous and fast-changing environments.
  • You can glean insights, translate concepts, deliver strategies.
  • You can manage vertical categories and horizontal interests.
  • You live in the future.
  • You are a flexible thinker and expert storyteller.
  • You pursue innovation in everything.
  • You are audacious.
  • You are a natural collaborator with a passion for excellence.
  • You are creative and detail-obsessed.
  • You move effortlessly across research, insights, models, frameworks, ideas,
  • and more.
  • You are looking for an opportunity to grow.
