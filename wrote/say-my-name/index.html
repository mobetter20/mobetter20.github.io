Say My Name

To whom it may concern

Originally published on Medium on December 23, 2022. View original

Dear mother of my child,
my wife,
my love,
my darling,
my star,
my sun and moon,
sola mio,
my lady,
my lass,
my gal,
my sweetheart,
my sunshine,
my hunny bunny,
my other half,
my better half,
my missus,
my one and only,

yes,
I’ve forgotten your name.
