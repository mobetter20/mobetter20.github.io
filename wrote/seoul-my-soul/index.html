Seoul My Soul

Why must you embarrass so, my soul?

Originally published on Medium on May 16, 2023. View original

‘Seoul, my soul’ selected as new city slogan. The city’s previous slogan, “I Seoul U,” also received criticism from the public regarding its inaccurate English grammar and its ambiguous meaning.

Are you saying
you
sold my soul?
or
sold my Seoul?

‘I SEOUL U’
really took a
toll on my soul

Now the new slogan
feels like
you’re just
trolling my soul

Seoul O’ Seoul,
why must you
embarrass
so our souls?

You make me
long to
bawl my soul
right out

Unfold my Seoul
from soulless politicking

Extol my Seoul
for its true essence,
but
‘Seoul my soul’,
it certainly is not.
